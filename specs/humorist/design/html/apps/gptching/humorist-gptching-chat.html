乾 Pinyin: qián

Pure creative force and initiating power. Leadership, vision, and continuous development.

Oracle

You have drawn Hexagram 1: Qián (The Creative) — the first and most powerful hexagram of the I Ching, representing pure yang energy.

This hexagram speaks of initiating power, creative potential, and the strength to begin great endeavors. Like the dragon rising through the heavens, now is a time for bold action and visionary thinking.

The oracle advises: Persist in what is right. Success comes through steady effort and unwavering integrity.
