Welcome to **The Humorist**, where artificial intelligence meets actual charm. We believe that the future of AI doesn't have to be sterile, robotic, or about maximizing engagement metrics.

## The Origin Story

Once upon a time, a group of bored engineers sat around a café debating an age-old question: "Why is talking to AI so... boring?"

> "Technology should amplify human connection and intelligence, not replace it."

## Our Mission

The Humorist exists at the intersection of **intelligence and personality**.

- **Empower:** Give you tools that make you smarter
- **Delight:** Make those tools genuinely enjoyable to use
- **Connect:** Help you engage with the world in a more human way
